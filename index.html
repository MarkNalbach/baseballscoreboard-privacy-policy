Privacy Policy for BaseballScoreboard App

Effective Date: January 5, 2025

Thank you for using BaseballScoreboard App! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our app, especially regarding advertising via AdMob.

1. Information We Collect

Currently, BaseballScoreboard does not directly collect, store, or process any personal information from users. However, our app uses **AdMob**, a third-party advertising service by Google, which may collect certain information for the purpose of serving personalized or non-personalized ads. This may include:

  • Device Information: This includes data such as IP address, device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers (e.g., Android ID, IDFA on iOS).
  • Ad Interaction Data: This includes data about how users interact with ads, such as clicks, impressions, and engagement metrics.
  • Location Data: Approximate location information, derived from IP address or GPS, may be collected for the purpose of serving location-based ads.

For more information on how AdMob collects and processes user data, please review Google's Privacy Policy and Google Ads Policies.

2. Third-Party Services (AdMob)

Our app uses AdMob by Google to display advertisements. AdMob may collect the following data for the purpose of serving personalized or non-personalized ads:

  • Device and Usage Information: This includes unique identifiers (e.g., device ID, advertising ID), device type, operating system, and app version.
  • Ad Interaction Data: Data on user engagement with ads, including impressions, clicks, and how users interact with ads within the app.
  • Location Information: Approximate geographic location derived from the IP address or GPS coordinates (if location services are enabled).

If personalized ads are enabled, Google may use this data for creating user profiles, targeting ads based on user interests, and improving the ad experience. For non-personalized ads, Google will use the collected data to serve generic ads based on the user's device and region.

3. How We Use Information

We do not collect or use any personal data directly. Data collected by AdMob is used for the following purposes:

  • Display Relevant Ads: AdMob uses the collected data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads based on user interests and location.
  • Measure Ad Performance: Data is used to measure the performance of ads, including impressions, clicks, and other engagement metrics.
  • Prevent Fraud: AdMob may use data to detect and prevent fraud and abuse related to the advertisements.

If you are in the EU or other regions with data privacy regulations, you may be required to provide consent for personalized ads before they are shown.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.
